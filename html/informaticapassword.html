What's your password? is it long or short? You should use different password for different website. You should use a password that is easy to memorise. A password should be long, so that it's harder for people to know your password. On this page, you're going to read about what password is the safest to use and more.

what is a good password? A traditional advice is:

  • a minimul of 12 characters
  • should include numbers, symbols, capital letters and lower-case letters.
  • isn't a dictionary word. Create your own word with a meaning.
  • doesn't rely on obvious situations, for example c1ty, you replaced the i with an 1.

you souldn't write your passwords on your phone. People can get acces to your phone and they may find out about all your passwords.
